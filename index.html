Career Opportunities

Software Engineer

Job Description :
We are looking for talented software engineers to design, develop, and implement innovative software solutions. As a software engineer at Waila Technology, you will play a key role in creating applications that solve real-world challenges and drive business growth.

Requirements :
Bachelor's or Master's degree in Computer Science, Software Engineering, or related field. Proficiency in programming languages like Java, Python, C++, etc. Strong problem-solving and analytical skills. Familiarity with Agile development methodologies.

UX/UI Designer

Job Description :
As a UX/UI Designer, you will collaborate with cross-functional teams to create intuitive and visually appealing user interfaces. Your designs will enhance user experience and drive engagement with our software products.

Requirements :
Proven experience in UX/UI design and proficiency with design tools such as Adobe XD, Sketch, Figma, etc.Understanding of user-centered design principles and best practices.Strong portfolio showcasing your design projects and problem-solving abilities.

Digital Marketer

Job Description :
Join our marketing team and be responsible for creating and executing digital marketing campaigns. You'll have the opportunity to drive brand awareness, lead generation, and customer engagement through various online channels.

Requirements :
Previous experience in digital marketing, including SEO, PPC, social media, email marketing, etc.Analytical mindset with the ability to measure and optimize campaign performance.Knowledge of marketing automation tools is a plus.
